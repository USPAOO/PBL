# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Campus community members — students, faculty, and staff — who buy and sell second-hand goods within their school. They use the platform on phones and laptops between classes, in dorms, or at work, often with limited time and attention. The primary job: quickly find trustworthy local deals, list items they no longer need, complete transactions safely, and communicate with buyers or sellers.

## Product Purpose

A campus second-hand marketplace ("校园二手") that keeps idle goods circulating within the school community. Success means fast discovery, low-friction listing and checkout, reliable order tracking, and direct messaging — all feeling local and trustworthy rather than like a generic e-commerce clone.

## Brand Personality

**年轻 · 可信 · 就近** — fresh energy with campus warmth.

Voice is upbeat and approachable, not corporate or salesy. The interface should feel like a lively campus bulletin board upgraded for mobile: familiar to anyone who has used 闲鱼, but clearly scoped to one school community. Emotions to evoke: confidence ("this is safe and nearby"), ease ("I can do this in two minutes"), and a touch of delight (discovering a good deal from someone on campus).

## Anti-references

- **廉价电商风**: full-screen promotions, screaming red/yellow sale banners, countdown timers, fake urgency, cluttered category grids.
- **Generic Ant Design defaults**: unmodified blue primary on gray `#f0f2f5` with no campus identity — acceptable as a starting point, not as the final character.
- **SaaS marketing clichés**: hero metrics, gradient text, glass cards, numbered section eyebrows (01 · 02 · 03).

## Design Principles

1. **Campus-first, not marketplace-generic** — every surface should signal "your school community" through copy, imagery tone, and localized trust cues, not through generic e-commerce patterns.
2. **Trust through clarity** — show seller identity, order status, and next actions plainly; never hide friction behind decoration.
3. **Speed is respect** — students and staff are busy; primary flows (browse → chat → buy → pay) must be reachable in minimal taps with obvious affordances.
4. **Energy without noise** — youthful and lively, but not cheap-promo loud; color and motion add personality, they don't compete with content.
5. **Practice what you preach** — if we say "让闲置流动起来", the UI should make listing and buying feel effortless, not like filling out a form.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target **WCAG 2.1 AA**: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, large text ≥3:1, visible focus states, keyboard-navigable menus and forms.
- Support **prefers-reduced-motion**: replace entrance animations with instant or crossfade transitions.
- Chinese UI copy throughout; ensure readable type at mobile sizes (≥16px body where possible).
